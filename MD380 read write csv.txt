MD380 read write csv to rdt codeplug

to convert rdt file to csv's files:
./rdt2csv -e UHF_General_Codeplug.rdt -sc -ch UHF_General_Channel.csv -cont UHF_General_Contact.csv -rxgrp UHF_General_RxGroup.csv -scan UHF_General_Scan.csv -zone UHF_General_Zone.csv

To upgrade csv files to rdt file:
./rdt2csv -u UHF_General_Codeplug.rdt -sc -ch UHF_General_Channel.csv -cont UHF_General_Contact.csv -rxgrp UHF_General_RxGroup.csv -scan UHF_General_Scan.csv -zone UHF_General_Zone.csv
